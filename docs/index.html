About Us

Indian Land Republican Women was formed to establish a republican presence in Indian Land, SC. A place where individuals can come to learn about politics in a friendly arena where we do not bash the other parties, instead, we set example of how republicans treat others. In hopes this openness will grow our party, increase local women candidates and increase turn out at the polls.
